BrandNewDay Privacy Policy & Support

Effective Date: 2026

BrandNewDay is a language learning app designed to help users practice vocabulary and learn languages including English, Spanish, Japanese, and Korean.

Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

The current version of BrandNewDay does not collect personal data from users.

The app does not transmit user information to our servers. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, photos, audio recordings, payment information, or learning records.

2. Local Account and Local Login

BrandNewDay may include a sign-up or login interface for creating a local learning profile.

This local profile is stored only on the user's device. The email or password entered in the app is not sent to any server and is not accessible by us.

3. Local Data Storage

Learning progress, vocabulary records, language preferences, and app settings are stored locally on the user's device.

We do not upload this information to a cloud server.

4. Third-Party Services

The current version of BrandNewDay does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, cloud storage services, or external login services to collect user data.

5. Children’s Privacy

BrandNewDay is designed for general language learning. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Future Updates

Future versions may add cloud accounts, server-based login, synchronization, or other online features. If these features are added, this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated accordingly.

BrandNewDay 隐私政策与技术支持

生效日期：2026 年

BrandNewDay 是一款语言学习 App，用于帮助用户练习词汇，并学习英语、西班牙语、日语和韩语。

隐私政策

1. 信息收集

当前版本的 BrandNewDay 不会收集用户的个人数据。

本 App 不会将用户信息传输到我们的服务器。我们不会收集用户姓名、邮箱、电话号码、位置信息、通讯录、照片、音频录音、支付信息或学习记录。

2. 本地账户与本地登录

BrandNewDay 可能包含注册或登录界面，用于创建本地学习资料。

该本地资料仅保存在用户设备本地。用户在 App 中输入的邮箱或密码不会发送到任何服务器，我们也无法访问这些信息。

3. 本地数据存储

学习进度、单词记录、语言偏好和 App 设置仅保存在用户设备本地。

我们不会将这些信息上传到云端服务器。

4. 第三方服务

当前版本的 BrandNewDay 不使用第三方分析工具、广告 SDK、云存储服务或外部登录服务来收集用户数据。

5. 儿童隐私

BrandNewDay 用于一般语言学习。我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

6. 后续更新

未来版本可能会加入云端账号、服务器登录、数据同步或其他在线功能。如果加入这些功能，我们会相应更新本隐私政策和 App Store 隐私信息。
