Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

BrandNewDay is designed to minimize data collection. We do not collect users’ personal learning records, location data, contacts, photos, audio recordings, or device files for advertising or tracking purposes.

The app does not require users to create an online account to use the core learning features. If the app includes a local profile, local login, or local learning identity, that information is stored on the user’s device and is not sent to our own server.

2. Local Data Storage

Learning progress, vocabulary records, language preferences, app settings, and local course progress may be stored locally on the user’s device.

We do not upload local learning records to our own cloud server. Users may remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from their device, unless future versions introduce optional cloud sync or account-based features.

3. Optional Plus / Pro Subscriptions

BrandNewDay may offer optional Plus and Pro membership subscriptions through Apple In-App Purchase.

Subscription payment, renewal, cancellation, refund handling, and billing management are processed by Apple. We do not directly collect, store, or process users’ payment card information.

The app may receive subscription status or entitlement information through Apple’s StoreKit system in order to unlock Plus or Pro features inside the app.

4. Support Email and Feedback

If users contact us by email for support, feedback, bug reports, Pro feature requests, language module requests, or custom vocabulary requests, we may receive the user’s email address and the content voluntarily included in the message.

We use this information only to respond to support requests, improve the app, and handle user-submitted feedback. We do not sell support emails or use them for third-party advertising.

5. AI-Assisted and Personalized Learning Features

BrandNewDay may include AI-assisted or personalized learning features to help users review vocabulary, organize learning content, or improve study efficiency.

Unless clearly stated inside the app, users’ personal learning records remain on the device and are not uploaded to our own server for AI processing. If future versions add server-based AI processing, cloud accounts, or synchronization features, this Privacy Policy and App Store privacy information will be updated accordingly.

6. Third-Party Services

At this time, BrandNewDay does not use third-party advertising SDKs or tracking tools to track users across other apps or websites.

Apple may process purchase, subscription, App Store, and payment-related information according to Apple’s own policies when users use App Store services or In-App Purchase.

7. Children’s Privacy

BrandNewDay is designed for general language learning. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law.

If a parent or guardian believes that a child has provided personal information to us through support email, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete the information.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information that users voluntarily send to us, such as support emails. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. Data Deletion Requests

Local learning data can generally be deleted by deleting the app from the user’s device.

If users have contacted us by email and want us to delete their support request or feedback message, they may contact us at the email address below.

10. Future Updates

Future versions of BrandNewDay may add optional cloud accounts, server-based login, synchronization, analytics, or online AI features. If our data practices change, we will update this Privacy Policy and the App Store privacy information.

BrandNewDay 隐私政策与技术支持

生效日期：2026 年 5 月 16 日

1. 我们收集的信息

BrandNewDay 的设计原则是尽量减少数据收集。本 App 不会为了广告或追踪目的收集用户的个人学习记录、位置信息、通讯录、照片、音频录音或设备文件。

用户无需创建在线账户即可使用核心学习功能。如果 App 中包含本地学习资料、本地登录或本地学习身份，这些信息仅保存在用户设备本地，不会发送到我们的服务器。

2. 本地数据存储

学习进度、单词记录、语言偏好、App 设置以及本地课程进度可能会保存在用户设备本地。

我们不会将本地学习记录上传到我们自己的云端服务器。除非未来版本加入可选的云同步或账号功能，否则用户通常可以通过删除 App 来清除本地保存的 App 数据。

3. Plus / Pro 会员订阅

BrandNewDay 可能会通过 Apple App 内购买系统提供可选的 Plus 和 Pro 会员订阅服务。

订阅付款、续费、取消、退款以及账单管理由 Apple 处理。我们不会直接收集、保存或处理用户的银行卡、信用卡或支付卡信息。

App 可能会通过 Apple StoreKit 系统接收订阅状态或权益信息，用于在 App 内解锁 Plus 或 Pro 功能。

4. 技术支持邮件与反馈

如果用户通过邮件联系我们，提交技术支持、问题反馈、Bug 报告、Pro 功能建议、语言模块请求或专属词库请求，我们可能会收到用户的邮箱地址以及用户在邮件中主动提交的内容。

除非 App 内明确说明，用户的个人学习记录会保留在设备本地，不会上传到我们的服务器进行 AI 处理。如果未来版本加入服务器端 AI、云账号或同步功能，我们会相应更新本隐私政策和 App Store 隐私信息。
